What is a plotter?
A "plotter" or "pen plotter" is a computer-controlled machine that holds a pen (or other mark-making implement) and draws onto a surface. Popular hobbyist plotters include the AxiDraw and the Makeblock XY.

What do people do with them?
Check out #plottertwitter on Twitter or #axidraw on Instagram for some examples!
